Snowboarding Instructor - Sunrise, AZ (Winter 2007 - 2008)

I signed up for the Coast Guard in November 2007 and was told I would be heading to bootcamp in May. Wildland Fire Fighter - Avra Valley, AZ (2006 - 2007)

I moved to Tucson Arizona after high school to search for more opportunity. I loved being a fire fighter but I didn't want to stay in my small town that I grew up in. I was able to work at Avra Valley fire department part time while also working full time for a tile installation company. The fire fighting work would require me to pack my bags and head out on 14 day assignements several times a year during the fire season. I worked on a small engine crew that would travel to places such as Utah, Montana and other parts of Arizona such as the north rim of the Grand Canyon to help fight wildland fires.

Fire Fighter - Pinetop/Lakeside Fire Dept., AZ (2003 - 2005)

When I was still a senior in high school, I had the opportunity to duel-enroll in the local community college that was offering classes that would lead me on a path to becoming a fire fighter. The college partnered with the local fire dept (which was just Pinetop Fire Dept. at the time before they merged with Lakeside) to train a new generation of fire fighters. The training was an absolutely amazing experienced that taught me everything from confined space rescue, to live fire excercises and even how to respond to serious car accidents. After the initial training I was able to work part time at the department as a "Red Shirt" (no not the Star Trek kind, thankfully) which gave me some exciting real world experience. Here I was also able to get wildland certified.
